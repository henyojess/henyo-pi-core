# Report Templates

## Template 1: Executive Brief (1-2 pages)

```markdown
# <Title> — Executive Brief

**Date:** <date>
**Topic:** <topic>
**Research Scope:** <what was covered>

---

## Key Findings

### 1. <Finding Headline>
<Evidence summary, 2-3 sentences. Cite [1-3].>

### 2. <Finding Headline>
...

## Implications
<Bullet points on what this means for the research question>

## Uncertainties
<Bullet points on what remains unclear or unverified>

## Conclusion
<Direct answer to the research question, 1 paragraph>

## Sources
[1] <source 1>
[2] <source 2>
```

```markdown

<How the research was conducted:
- Data sources
- Search strategies
- Timeframe of research
- Inclusion/exclusion criteria>

## 3. Findings

### 3.1 <Subtopic 1>
<Detailed findings with citations>

### 3.2 <Subtopic 2>
...

### 3.3 <Subtopic 3>
...

## 4. Analysis
<Cross-cutting themes, patterns, and insights that emerge from the findings>

### 4.1 <Pattern 1>
### 4.2 <Pattern 2>

## 5. Comparison
<If applicable: compare different approaches, viewpoints, or datasets>

## 6. Discussion
<Interpretation of findings, implications, and limitations>

<Direct, comprehensive answer to the research question>

## References

...

## Appendix A: Detailed Evidence
<Full evidence cards or tables>

## Appendix B: Search Log
<Summary of searches conducted>

## Appendix C: Glossary
<Terminology definitions for complex topics>
```

## Citation Format Examples

### Academic Paper
```
[1] J. Smith et al., "Title of Paper," *Journal Name*, vol. X, no. Y, pp. Z-Z, 2025.
    DOI: 10.xxxx/xxxxx | URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/XXXX.XXXXX
```

### Web Article
```
[2] A. Author, "Title of Article," *Publication*, Jan 15, 2025.
    URL: https://example.com/article | Accessed: Jul 9, 2025
```

### Company Report
```
[3] Company Name, "Report Title," *Report Type*, Q1 2025.
    URL: https://company.com/reports/report.pdf
```

### Social Media (Expert Opinion)
```
[4] @handle, "Post text," *Platform*, Jun 1, 2025.
    URL: https://platform.com/@handle/post-id
```

### Dataset
```
[5] Organization, "Dataset Name," YYYY.
    URL: https://url-to-data | Version: X.Y
```
